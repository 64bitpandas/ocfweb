Request Berkeley.edu Subdomain

One of the benefits of hosting your student group's site with the OCF is that you have the option of using an address like "mygroup.berkeley.edu" to access your site. However, in order to obtain a subdomain for your site, you must meet both university policies regarding acceptable use of berkeley.edu subdomains, as well as OCF policies for virtual hosting.

Please start by reading the following documents in their entirety:

  • Virtual Hosting (OCF)
  • UC Berkeley Computer Use Policy
  • UC Berkeley DNS Service Policy

If you believe your group satisfies all requirements listed in the above documents, you may complete the form below to request for us to set up a virtual host for your account.
