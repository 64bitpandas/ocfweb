The Open Computing Facility is an all-volunteer student organization located at the University of California, Berkeley. Among other services, we offer:

  • A spiffy computer lab in 6A Hearst Gym
  • Free printing for all UC Berkeley students
  • Shell accounts on our powerful on-campus servers

We hold weekly staff hours to provide assistance with account issues or with OCF services. Drop-ins welcome!
