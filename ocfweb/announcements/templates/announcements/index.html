About the Open Computing Facility

The OCF is an all-volunteer student organization.

Our staff work hard to provide valuable services to the campus community:

  • A spiffy computer lab in 171 MLK
  • Web, application, and email hosting for over 80% of student groups and thousands of individual students.
  • Free printing, used by over 5,000 students per semester.
